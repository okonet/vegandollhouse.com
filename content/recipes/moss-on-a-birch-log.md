#### Moss on a Birch Log ingredients:

- 1 stalk celery
- vegan cream cheese (I like the Daiya chive & onion)
- capers

#### ![](/images/birch-logs.jpg)



#### Moss on a Birch Log directions:

**Spread** the celery with cream cheese.

**Sprinkle** with capers.
